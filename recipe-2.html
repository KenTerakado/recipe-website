Chicken Alfredo

  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredients

  • 200mg of boiled water
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshley grated parmesan
  • Freshley chopped parsley
  • 8 oz fettuccine

Directions

  1. Step 0

    This is step 0 of the recipe.

  2. Step 1 - In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil.
  3. Step 2 - Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook until golden and cooked through, 8 minutes per side. Let rest 10 minutes, then slice.
  4. Step 3 - Add milk, broth, and garlic to skillet.
  5. Step 4 - Season with salt and pepper and bring to a simmer.
  6. Step 5 - Add fettuccine, stirring frequently for about 3 minutes. Let cook until al dente, 8 minutes more.
  7. Step 6 - Stir in heavy cream and Parmesan until combined. Simmer until sauce thickens.
  8. Step 7 - Remove from heat and stir in sliced chicken. Garnish with parsley.
